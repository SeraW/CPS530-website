Why Cereal is Better without Milk

Wet Cereal Sucks

It's soggy and it makes the texture uneven. Cereal was made to be crunchy; if you add milk, you are obligated to rush to eat it before it turns into complete mush.

Milk No Taste

Milk adds nothing but pain to the experience. If you like the soggy texture, you might as well eat it with water
